Our Programs

What we offer

Skills Clinics
Everyone does skill development camps. While we offer those, we promise they will be unique experiences that will change the way players think about the game.  These clinics will give you tools to constantly chase excellence in your skills without a gym or a coach.  

Recruiting Seminars
We offer recruiting seminars that provide real life stories about the recruiting process and how it works from a college coach's perspective.  This isn't a sales pitch to get you to sign up for a recruiting service.  We give you information that will help you on your journey to the next level. 

Strength and Conditioning Clinics
Chasing excellence is more than just the sport itself.  It's about being a better athlete too.  Being a better athlete for your sport helps you chase your excellence in a completely different way.  These sessions will empower you to chase excellence in your every day workouts with or without someone else's help.

Nutrition Seminars
If your body is a machine, the food you eat is the fuel for your machine.  The better you take care of your body, the better you will perform. It is difficult to run full speed after your excellence if you're not fueling your machine appropriately. These sessions will give you real information about chasing excellence in your diet. 

Top of the Line Facilities and Equipment
We want our athletes to have access to the best possible facilities and equipment that we can possibly provide to them. All you really need is a ball and a basket, but closing those small gaps can be the difference in having access to the right training tool or not. 




01.

Go Fast!

If you're chasing something, you better move at full speed. If you're not moving at full speed, you're not moving at game speed.  This is the only way you get to where you're going. It's the only way to truly get better. 

02.

Fail Often!

When you go fast, you'll probably mess up.  If you're doing it perfectly, do it faster.  You'll probably mess up again and that's where the real growth comes in.  The more you mess up, the faster you'll catch excellence.

03.

Have Fun!

If you're not having fun, then why are you doing it? Failing isn't fun, but the chase of excellence should be a blast. When you learn to embrace failure as an opportunity to learn, you are truly in a chase for excellence. 

About Us

We are basketball nerds. We love the game and what it brings. From the competition of games, the grind of daily practice, film study, the mental side, physical conditioning and everything in between. We are two individuals that will meet a player wherever they are and try to help them get to where they say they want to go.

We have been around the game of basketball for over half of our lives. Basketball has given both of us the opportunity to meet some incredible people from all over the world. Basketball brought us together and gave us the opportunity to form a relationship that will now last a lifetime. It has brought some of the highest highs but also some of the toughest lows. It has taught us how to be better at life and how to be better humans.

It is now our mission to be able to give and share those experiences with others. We are passionate about what we do. We know that if you are willing to live a relentless life and chase excellence in all that you do you will have many unique opportunities far beyond basketball.

Youth in today’s society are becoming those of the haves and the have nots. We believe there should be NO have nots; everyone should have an opportunity to a path of greatness regardless of race, gender, age, or social status. We are leading by example to show that you can have what you want if you are willing to not only work hard but also smart outside of your comfort zone. Along the way in life everyone has a dream so big that they need help, asking for it is the hard part.

We are about honesty, integrity, hard work, creativity, passion, truth, love, and growth through providing opportunities for others. We use basketball as a way to love others. It brings us together with people that we may have otherwise never had the opportunity to meet, much less build a relationship with and impact their lives. We are all about serving others; we know we have such short amount of time on this earth so while we are here we are all about making the most of it by loving others through basketball.

We believe that everyone is flawed. We believe perfection is impossible and in trying to be perfect we stunt our growth and development.  We believe that we all can be excellent if we want to be.  We strive to provide opportunities to everyone regardless of gender, race, religion, or any other social classification so that they have the tools to be excellent if they choose to chase it. We do not have a magic pill.  We only possess the recipe and the roadmap to help others achieve excellence in their journey.  Do you want to chase excellence? We do it every day.  Join us and let's chase excellence together.

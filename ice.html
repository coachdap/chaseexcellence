About ICE

I CHASE EXCELLENCE (ICE) provides thousands of dollars for children who couldn't otherwise have access to the facilities, programs and expertise provided through Chase Excellence and Hoops College. As part of ICE, you help participants learn what it takes to be their best both on and off the court.  Along with your impact on society, you get all the benefits that come with being a part of this organization.
